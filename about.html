Our Story

Founded in 2024, Sweet Delights Bakery began as a dream shared by RTC G6 to create a haven where sweetness and creativity collide.
With a passion for baking and a dedication to quality, our founder set out to redefine the dessert experience.
What started as a humble kitchen experiment quickly blossomed into a beloved local bakery, cherished by the community for our commitment to excellence.
